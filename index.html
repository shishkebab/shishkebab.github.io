Hi! I am Sukwha, currently a Ph.D. student in computer science (Cybersecurity) at the Laboratory of Security Engineering for Future Computing (SEFCOM) at Arizona State University. I am under the supervision of Dr. Gail-Joon Ahn, Dr. Adam Doupé, Dr. Yan Shoshitaishvili, Dr. Tiffany Bao, and Dr. Ruoyu (Fish) Wang.
My research area includes security & privacy in software-defined networks (SDN), mobile networks (LTE, 5G), and cyber deception.

Email: skyung1@asu.edu
